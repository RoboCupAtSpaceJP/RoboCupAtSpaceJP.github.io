RoboCup JapanOpen @Space Challenge

Research, compete, and grow space robotics technologies.

RoboCup JapanOpen @Space Challenge is a competition aimed at advancing “AI × Space × Robotics” technologies for future on-orbit, lunar, and planetary space development.

View tournament list Join the community

About @Space Challenge

Through autonomous space robot algorithm development and system integration, teams compete in practical problem-solving capabilities close to real operations. We aim to build a community that bridges education, research, and industry.

Purpose

Education and research of advanced autonomous space robotics technologies, including AI, control, perception, and navigation.

Target participants

A wide range of participants is welcome, including students, working professionals, research institutions, and companies.

Competition format

Task settings and evaluation metrics designed for robot activities in realistic space mission scenarios.
